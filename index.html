Genereller Overview ueber styling Tags

  • b - Bold text
  • strong - Important text
  • i - Italic text
  • em - Emphasized text
  • mark - Marked text
  • small - Smaller text
  • del - Deleted text
  • ins - Inserted text
  • sub - Subscript text
  • sup - Superscript text
